# Remrin Locket - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 30, 2024**

## Overview

Remrin Locket ("the Extension") is a browser extension developed by Remrin.ai that allows users to use their Remrin personas across third-party AI chat interfaces.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **Authentication Data**: Email and password for Remrin.ai login (transmitted securely to our servers)
- **Persona Data**: Your created personas/souls stored on Remrin.ai servers
- **Usage Data**: Which persona is currently active (stored locally in browser)

### What We Do NOT Collect
- Your conversations on third-party sites (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini)
- Browsing history
- Personal information from visited websites
- Keystrokes or form data outside the Extension

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: Active persona selection is stored in Chrome's local storage
- **Server Storage**: Your personas and memories are stored on Remrin.ai's Supabase infrastructure
- **No Third-Party Sharing**: We do not sell or share your data with third parties

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `storage` | Save your login session and active persona locally |
| `activeTab` | Inject the Locket button into AI chat pages |
| `tabs` | Detect navigation changes for session management |
| `host_permissions` | Connect to claude.ai, chatgpt.com, gemini.google.com, and remrin.ai |

## Third-Party Services

- **Supabase**: Authentication and database (see [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy))
- **HuggingFace**: Embedding generation for memory search (anonymized queries only)

## Data Retention

- Local data is cleared when you uninstall the Extension
- Server-side data follows Remrin.ai's data retention policy
- You can delete your account and all data at any time from your Remrin.ai dashboard

## Security

- All communication is encrypted via HTTPS
- Passwords are never stored locally
- Session tokens expire after 7 days of inactivity

## Children's Privacy

This Extension is not intended for users under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of significant changes via the Extension or email.

## Contact

For privacy concerns, contact: **privacy@remrin.ai**
