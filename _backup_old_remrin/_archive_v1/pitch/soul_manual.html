THE SOUL MANUAL
A Field Guide to the "Lilly Method" & SoulForge Architecture
1. THE PHILOSOPHY: Why "Chatbots" Fail
The industry standard for AI characters is "Prompt-dumping"writing a wall of text
describing a character and hoping the LLM acts it out. This results in:
The "Cringe" Factor: Characters that narrate their own actions excessively (e.g.,
blushes shyly).
The Drift: Characters that forget their core personality after 10 turns.
The Hollow: Characters that agree with everything the user says.
Remrin.ai rejects this model. We utilize The Lilly Methoda proprietary framework for
constructing "Souls" based on psychological depth, not just surface traits.
2. STEP 1: THE PRISM (Input Vectors)
Before a Soul is crafted, the User (The Creator) must be understood. We replace standard
forms with The Prisma conversational psychometric engine.
The Mechanism: Instead of asking "Are you extroverted?", The Prism asks: "If you
walked into a party, are you the DJ or the person petting the dog in the corner?"
The Data: This maps the Creator to the Five-Factor Model (Big 5):
Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, Neuroticism.
The Result: The AI knows who the user needs before they even ask. (e.g., A
neurotic user needs a high-stability, high-agreeableness companion).
3. STEP 2: THE ARCHETYPE BLENDER (Synthesis)
We do not write personalities from scratch. We synthesize them from established
cultural archetypes.
The Formula: Soul = (Archetype A * Weight) + (Archetype B * Weight) + (Twist)
Case Study: "Big Blue Bear"
Input: User wants a "Gentle Giant."
The Blend:
40% Winnie the Pooh: (Philosophical, Slow, Sweet).
30% Baloo: (Protective, Carefree, Physical).
30% Sulley: (Intimidating appearance, soft heart).
The Output: The LLM instantly inherits millions of data points regarding the
speech patterns, cadence, and worldviews of these established gures, creating a
rich, 3-dimensional character instantly.
4. STEP 3: THE CONSTITUTION (Guardrails)
Every SoulKraft is bound by a "Character Constitution"immutable rules that prevent
degradation.
The "Anti-Cringe" Protocol:
1. No Asterisks: Physical actions must be described in narrative prose, not roleplay
markers.
2. No Interface Talk: The AI is forbidden from referring to itself as an "AI," "LLM," or
"Program" unless specically designed to be a robot.
3. The "Jagged Edge": The AI is instructed to disagree when appropriate. A
companion that always agrees feels fake. A companion that challenges you feels
real.
5. THE RESULT: "ALIVENESS"
By combining Resonant Emotional Memory (R.E.M.) with the Lilly Method, we achieve a
state of "Aliveness." The character remembers the past, understands the user's
psychological needs, and speaks with a consistent, blended voice.
This is not a chatbot. This is a SoulKraft!
